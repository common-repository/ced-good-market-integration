CedCommerce Integration for Good Market extension aims to help merchants automate their product, order & inventory management from a single dashboard. 


== Description ==

**CedCommerce integration for Good Market** is an all-in-one solution for merchants to establish a perfect synchronization between their WooCommerce store and Good Market. The extension aims to help merchants automate their product, order & inventory management from a single dashboard.

Furthermore, the integration app allows merchants to map or configure desired Good Market product categories with WooCommerce product categories from the app itself, and that too, in no time. Hence, saving your precious time for other important tasks. 


= Main features =

* **Profile-based product upload -** Create profiles as per your requirements and list products in them, and auto-upload products based on profiles. 
* **Effortless product management -** Enjoy an easy and quick listing of WooCommerce products on Good Market. Fetch or update product details such as the product title, description, images, and so on from the app itself in just a few clicks.
* **Order management -** With the Good Market Integration app, you can easily manage your WooCommerce as well as Good Market orders from the app itself. You can keep track of the order fulfillment process without juggling between the platforms.
* **Bulk operations -** No more deleting or uploading bulk products one by one, instead select the entire lot and perform the action as needed. The app’s bulk uploads & delete feature is as simple as its name.
* **Product Category Mapping -** Simplify the process of mapping product categories of your WooCommerce store with Good Market categories from the app itself.
* **Instant notification -** As soon as any order is received on Good Market, you will get an instant notification for the same via email. This way you will never miss any order and you will be able to fulfill all your orders timely.
* **Real-time Synchronization -** Stay up-to-date with all the latest updates, as the app synchronizes information in real time. For this, merchants need to enable the auto-sync feature.

== Installation ==

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Product Auto Share, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "CedCommerce integration for Good Market" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our CedCommerce integration for Good Market plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our CedCommerce integration for Good Market plugin and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work like a charm; as always though, ensure you backup your site just in case.
